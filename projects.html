blair3sat

CubeSats are miniaturized satellites which usually come in sizes of 1U to 6U, each unit measuring approximately 10 x 10 x 10 cubic centimeters, and provide a platform to demonstrate smaller scale missions. You can read more about our team at our website.


ISS attitude determination

I worked with near real-time orbit data from the USGNC (U.S. Guidance, Navigation, and Control) system to determine the field of view of an instrument installed on the International Space Station. You can find my report on arXiv.
