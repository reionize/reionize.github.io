Projects



Long-range decoherence

I investigated the decoherence rate due to long-range interactions under the guidance of Prof. Jacob Taylor and Dr. Daniel Carney at the Joint Quantum Institute. This work has potential implications for quantum gravity, in particular making testable predictions for tabletop experiments.


Tetraquarks

I numerically modeled the binding energy of doubly heavy tetraquarks to make predictions in the heavy quark mass limit under the guidance of Prof. Thomas Cohen at the Maryland Center for Fundamental Physics. You can read my abstract here.


blair3sat

CubeSats are miniaturized satellites which usually come in sizes of 1U to 6U, each unit measuring approximately 10 x 10 x 10 cubic centimeters, and provide a platform to demonstrate smaller scale missions. You can read more about our team at our website.
